Ella Day Tour

Duration

Starting Location: Ella

Duration: Full Day

Morning

7:00 AM: Start your day Your guide will meet you at your accommodation in Ella.

7:30 AM: Little Adam’s Peak Hike - Enjoy the easy trail with panoramic views.

9:00 AM: Nine Arches Bridge - Take photos amidst lush greenery.

10:30 AM: Ravana Falls - Relax or take a dip if conditions allow.

Afternoon

12:00 PM: Lunch Break - Savor Sri Lankan dishes at a local restaurant.

1:30 PM: Ella Spice Garden (Optional) - Learn about Sri Lanka’s famous spices.

2:30 PM: Ella Rock Hike (Optional or Short Trail) - Stunning views or explore the local town.

Evening

4:30 PM: Relax at 98 Acres Resort (Optional) - Enjoy tea or snacks.

6:00 PM: Drop-off at your accommodation - Conclude your memorable day.

Tour Includes

  • Transportation (if needed for attractions)
  • English-speaking guide
  • Bottled water

What to Bring

  • Comfortable hiking shoes
  • Sunblock, hat, and sunglasses
  • Camera to capture the stunning scenery
  • Swimwear for Ravana Falls (optional)
