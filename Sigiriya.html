Sigiriya Day Tour Itinerary

Discover the beauty and culture of Sri Lanka

Duration

Full DayPickup Location: Kandy

Duration: pre-arranged location

Early morning

6:00 AM Departure from KandyBegin your day with an early pickup from your hotel in Kandy. Sit back and enjoy a scenic drive through Sri Lanka’s lush landscapes.

8:00 AM: Stop for Breakfast (Optional)Enjoy a traditional Sri Lankan breakfast or grab a quick meal at a local restaurant en route

morning

9:30 AM : Sigiriya Rock FortressArrive at the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, also known as the “Lion Rock.” Climb to the top to marvel at breathtaking views and explore the ancient palace ruins, frescoes, and water gardens.

11:30 AM : Sigiriya MuseumDelve deeper into the history and archaeology of Sigiriya by visiting the museum, which showcases artifacts and the story of the fortress.

Afternoon

12:30 PM : Traditional Sri Lankan LunchEnjoy a delicious Sri Lankan lunch at a nearby restaurant, featuring local flavors and specialties.

2:00 PM : Village Tour (Optional)Experience authentic Sri Lankan rural life with a village tour. Ride a bullock cart, paddle a traditional canoe, and enjoy a local cooking demonstration.

2:00 PM :Visit the Dambulla Cave TempleExplore this UNESCO World Heritage Site, famous for its stunning rock caves adorned with ancient murals and Buddha statues.

Evening

5:00 PM:Return Journey to KandyRelax as you drive back to Kandy, reminiscing about your adventure-packed day.
