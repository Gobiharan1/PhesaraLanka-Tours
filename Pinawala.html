Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage Tour

Discover the beauty and culture of Sri Lanka

Duration

Starting Location: Kandy

Duration: Half-Day

Morning

8:00 AM : Departure from KandyBegin your journey with a comfortable drive from Kandy to Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, located approximately 1.5 hours away. Enjoy the scenic countryside along the way.

9:30 AM: Arrival at Pinnawala Elephant OrphanageReach the world-renowned Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, a sanctuary dedicated to the care of orphaned, injured, and abandoned elephants.

10:00 AM:Elephant Feeding SessionWitness the heartwarming morning feeding session. Watch as the elephants, including playful calves, are hand-fed milk and fruit by the caretakers.

11:00 AM: Elephant Bathing in the RiverExperience the highlight of the tour as the elephants are led to the Maha Oya River for their daily bath. Marvel at the majestic sight of elephants splashing, playing, and relaxing in the water. This is a perfect opportunity for photos and videos.

8:00 AM : Explore the Orphanage GroundsTake a leisurely walk around the orphanage. Learn about the conservation efforts and the stories of the elephants from guides or information boards

Afternoon

1:00 PM : Lunch BreakEnjoy lunch at a nearby restaurant overlooking the river or in a local eatery offering traditional Sri Lankan cuisine.

2:30 PM: Optional Stop at Spice Garden or Handicraft ShopsOn your way back, you can opt to visit a spice garden to learn about Sri Lanka’s rich history in spices or stop at local shops selling handicrafts and souvenirs.

4:00 PM : Return to KandyHead back to Kandy, arriving by late afternoon, with wonderful memories of your time spent with the gentle giants of Pinnawala.

Tour Includes

  • Comfortable round-trip transportation
  • Entrance tickets to all attractions English-speaking guide

What to Bring

  • Camera to capture unforgettable moments Comfortable shoes for walking Hat and sunscreen for sunny weather
  • Discover the joy of connecting with these magnificent creatures and take home a story worth sharing!
