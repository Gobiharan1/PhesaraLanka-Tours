Kandy Day Tour

Duration

Pickup Location: Kandy

Duration: full day

Morning

8:00 AM: Pickup from Your Hotel Start your day with a warm welcome from your friendly guide and driver.

8:30 AM:Visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth RelicExplore Sri Lanka’s most sacred Buddhist temple, where a relic of Lord Buddha is enshrined. Learn about its rich history and cultural significance.

10:00 AM:Royal Botanical Gardens, Peradeniya Wander through beautifully landscaped gardens featuring over 4,000 species of plants, including orchids, spices, and towering palms.

11:30 AM: Bahirawakanda Buddha Statue Enjoy panoramic views of Kandy city fromthis iconic hilltop Buddha statue.

Afternoon

12:00 PM: Traditional Sri Lankan LunchSavor a delicious Sri Lankan meal at a local restaurant.

2:00 PM: Kandy Lake and City Walk Take a stroll around the serene Kandy Lake and visit the lively city center to explore local shops, markets, and handicrafts.

3:30 PM: Gem Museum and Workshop Learn about Sri Lanka’s world-famous gemstones, their mining process, and intricate craftsmanship

4:30 PM: Ceylon Tea Factory Tour Discover the process of making Sri Lanka’s finest teas, from leaf to cup, and enjoy a refreshing tea tasting

Evening

5:30 PM: Kandyan Cultural Dance Performance Experience a vibrant display of Sri Lanka’s traditional Kandyan dance and drumming.

7:00 PM: Return to Your Hotel Your guide will drop you off at your hotel, concluding a memorable day in the heart of Sri Lanka.

Tour Includes

  • Transportation in an air-conditioned vehicle
  • English-speaking guide Entry tickets to main attractions (optional, based on package selection)
  • Bottled water

What to Bring

  • Comfortable hiking shoes
  • Sunblock, hat, and sunglasses
  • Camera to capture the stunning scenery
