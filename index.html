Your Gateway to Sri Lanka’s Wonders

Welcome to Pehesara Lanka Tours, where unforgettable adventures meet seamless travel experiences. As a passionate and reliable inbound tour agency, we are dedicated to unveiling the magic of Sri Lanka through curated tours that celebrate its rich heritage, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality. Whether you’re exploring the majestic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, soaking in the tranquility of lush tea estates, or embarking on thrilling wildlife safaris, our expertly designed day tours and round tours cater to all your travel desires.
