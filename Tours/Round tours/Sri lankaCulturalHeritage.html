Sri Lanka Cultural Heritage Tour

Discover the beauty and culture of Sri Lanka

Overview

Embark on a journey through Sri Lanka’s fascinating history and culture. This tour will take you to the island’s most iconic ancient sites, sacred temples, and vibrant cultural hubs. Perfect for history buffs and those seeking a deeper connection with Sri Lanka’s heritage

Highlights

Photograph the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress and Dambulla Cave Temples Witness the sunrise over the lush tea plantations in Nuwara Eliya Explore the vibrant streets and colonial charm of Galle Fort Capture the beauty of wildlife at Yala National Park Scenic train ride through misty hills and waterfalls

Arrival in Colombo – Transfer to Negombo

  • Meet and greet at Bandaranaike International Airport.
  • Drive to Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka’s ancient capital.
  • Explore the sacred Bodhi Tree (Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi) and ancient stupas.

Anuradhapura to Sigiriya

  • DMorning drive to Sigiriya.
  • Visit Sigiriya Rock Fortress and marvel at its ancient frescoes and gardens.
  • Overnight stay in a cultural eco-lodge.

Polonnaruwa Ancient City Tour

  • Day trip to Polonnaruwa to explore ancient ruins, statues, and the Parakrama Samudra reservoir.
  • Return to Sigiriya in the evening.

Dambulla to Kandy

  • Visit the Dambulla Cave Temples adorned with intricate murals and Buddha statues.
  • Free time to enjoy the beach or explore local cafes and shops.
  • Evening visit to the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic.

Cultural Day in Kandy

  • Morning stroll through the Royal Botanical Gardens in Peradeniya.
  • Witness a traditional Kandyan cultural dance performance in the evening.

Kandy to Galle

  • Drive to Galle, stopping at Ambalangoda to visit a traditional mask-making workshop.
  • Explore the historic Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Departure from Colombo

  • Transfer to Colombo for a city tour or shopping, depending on your flight time.
  • Airport drop-off for your return flight.

Inclusions

  • Accommodation in heritage hotels and eco-lodges
  • Daily breakfast and one cultural dinner in Kandy
  • Private transportation in an air-conditioned vehicle
  • Entrance fees to all cultural and historical sites
  • Guided tours at key locations
