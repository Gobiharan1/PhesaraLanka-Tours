Sri Lanka Beach Tour

Overview

Explore the epic tales Sri Lanka Beach TouEscape to Sri Lanka’s sun-kissed shores and indulge in the ultimate beach vacation. From golden sandy beaches to turquoise waters, this tour offers relaxation, adventure, and breathtaking coastal views. Perfect for sunseekers and water sports enthusiasts alike.

Highlights

Relax on pristine beaches at Bentota, Mirissa, and Unawatuna Enjoy water sports like snorkeling, diving, and jet skiing Experience a magical whale-watching excursion in Mirissa Visit the iconic Galle Fort and coastal landmarks Savor fresh seafood and tropical delicacies.

Arrival in Colombo – Transfer to Negombo

  • Meet and greet at Bandaranaike International Airport.
  • VRelax at Negombo Beach and explore the nearby Dutch Canal.

Negombo to Bentota

  • Drive to Bentota, a paradise for water sports.
  • Enjoy activities like jet skiing, banana boating, or windsurfing.
  • Visit a turtle hatchery and learn about sea turtle conservation.

Bentota to Mirissa

  • Drive along the scenic southern coast to Mirissa
  • Evening relaxation on the beach with stunning sunset views.

Whale Watching and Leisure

  • Early morning whale-watching excursion in Mirissa.
  • Free time to enjoy the beach or explore local cafes and shops.

Mirissa to Unawatuna via Galle

  • Stop at Galle to visit the historic Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Continue to Unawatuna for a relaxing beach experience.

Explore Unawatuna and Jungle Beach

  • Enjoy snorkeling at Jungle Beach or take a short hike for panoramic views.
  • Relax and soak up the tropical vibes at Unawatuna Beach.

Departure from Colombo

  • Scenic drive back to Colombo.
  • Optional city tour or shopping stop before airport drop-off.
