Arrival in Colombo – Transfer to Negombo

  • Meet and greet at Bandaranaike International Airport.
  • Check-in at a beachside resort in Negombo.
  • Evening at leisure to enjoy a romantic sunset

Negombo to Kandy

  • Scenic drive to Kandy, Sri Lanka’s cultural capital.
  • Visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic.
  • Romantic evening stroll around Kandy Lake.

Kandy to Nuwara Eliya

  • Train ride through picturesque tea plantations
  • Visit a tea factory and learn about tea-making
  • Relax at a colonial-style hotel

Nuwara Eliya to Yala National Park

  • Drive to Yala, stopping at Ravana Falls.
  • Private safari in Yala to spot leopards and elephants.
  • Overnight stay in a luxury safari lodge.

Yala to Mirissa

  • Head to Mirissa for a relaxing beach stay.
  • Romantic beach dinner under the stars.

Whale Watching and Galle Fort Visit

  • Morning whale-watching tour.
  • Explore the historic Galle Fort in the afternoon..

Departure from Colombo

  • Transfer to Colombo for your return flight.
