Sri Lanka Photography Tour

Overview

Capture the essence of Sri Lanka’s breathtaking landscapes, vibrant culture, and diverse wildlife through your lens. Designed for photography enthusiasts, this tour takes you to the most photogenic locations on the island, providing endless opportunities to create stunning memories

Highlights

Photograph the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress and Dambulla Cave Temples Witness the sunrise over the lush tea plantations in Nuwara Eliya Explore the vibrant streets and colonial charm of Galle Fort Capture the beauty of wildlife at Yala National Park Scenic train ride through misty hills and waterfalls

Arrival in Colombo – Negombo

  • Meet and greet at Bandaranaike International Airport.
  • Visit Negombo’s fishing village and lagoon for sunset photography.

Negombo to Sigiriya

  • Drive to Sigiriya and visit the famous Lion Rock Fortres
  • Capture panoramic views and ancient frescoes during the golden hour..

Dambulla and Kandy

  • Visit the Dambulla Cave Temples and capture intricate murals and statues. Drive to Kandy, photographing lush greenery and traditional architecture en route
  • Evening visit to the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic for cultural shots.

Scenic Train Ride to Nuwara Eliya

  • Morning train ride from Kandy to Nuwara Eliya, passing tea plantations and waterfalls.
  • Sunset photography at Gregory Lake or nearby tea estates.

Nuwara Eliya to Ella

  • Drive to Ella, stopping at Ramboda Falls for dramatic waterfall shots.
  • Visit Nine Arches Bridge and capture its iconic structure amidst lush greenery.

Ella to Yala National Park

  • Morning hike to Little Adam’s Peak for sunrise photography.
  • Drive to Yala National Park for a wildlife safari, focusing on leopards, elephants, and birdlife

Yala to Galle

  • Drive to Galle, stopping at stilt fishing spots for unique cultural photographs.
  • Explore Galle Fort and capture its colonial charm and vibrant streets.

Galle to Colombo – Departure

  • Return to Colombo and explore the city’s modern and historic landmarks.
  • Airport drop-off for your departure flight.
