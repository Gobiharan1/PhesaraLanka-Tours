Sri Lanka Wildlife Tour

Overview

Dive into the heart of Sri Lanka's incredible biodiversity with this thrilling wildlife tour. From majestic elephants to elusive leopards, this journey offers unforgettable encounters with nature in its purest form.

Highlights

Jeep safaris in iconic national parks (Yala, Udawalawe, Minneriya) Visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage Birdwatching at Bundala National Park Exploration of Sinharaja Rainforest Reserve (UNESCO World Heritage Site) Relaxing stay in eco-friendly lodge

Arrival in Colombo – Transfer to Sigiriya

  • Meet and greet at Bandaranaike International Airport.
  • Drive to Sigiriya and check into a wildlife-themed eco-lodge
  • Evening safari at Minneriya National Park to witness the famous Elephant Gathering.Day

Sigiriya to Kandy

  • Morning visit to Sigiriya Rock Fortress.
  • Drive to Kandy, visiting the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage en route..
  • Explore the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic in the evening.

Kandy to Udawalawe National Park

  • Scenic drive to Udawalawe.
  • Afternoon safari to spot elephants and other wildlife in their natural habitat..
  • Overnight stay in a cozy wildlife lodge.

Udawalawe to Yala National Park

  • Drive to Yala, Sri Lanka’s premier wildlife destination.
  • Evening safari in Yala National Park for a chance to see leopards and sloth bears.

Bundala National Park and Relaxation

  • Morning birdwatching tour at Bundala National Park.
  • Afternoon at leisure in Yala.

Yala to Sinharaja Rainforest

  • Transfer to the Sinharaja Rainforest Reserve.
  • Guided nature walk to explore the rich flora and fauna..

Departure from Colombo

  • Scenic drive back to Colombo.
  • Optional city tour or shopping stop before airport drop-off.
