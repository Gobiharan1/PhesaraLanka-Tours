Nuwara Eliya Day Tour

Discover the beauty and culture of Sri Lanka

Duration

Starting Location: Kandy

Duration: Full-Day

Morning

7:30 AM: Departure from KandySet off early from Kandy with a scenic drive through winding roads, lush tea plantations, and misty hills.

9:00 AM: Visit Ramboda FallsMake a stop at the picturesque Ramboda Falls. Enjoy the beauty of cascading water surrounded by greenery, perfect for photography..

10:00 AM:Explore a Tea Plantation & FactoryVisit a renowned tea estate to witness the tea-making process, from plucking fresh leaves to crafting the final brew. Enjoy a freshly brewed cup of Ceylon tea and learn about the history of Sri Lanka’s tea industry.

11:30 AM: Arrival in Nuwara EliyaReach the charming town of Nuwara Eliya, often referred to as “Little England” for its colonial charm and cool climate.

Afternoon

12:00 PM: Gregory Lake VisitTake a leisurely walk around the scenic Gregory Lake. Optional activities include paddle boating, pony rides, or simply relaxing by the lakeside.

1:00 PM: Lunch in Nuwara EliyaSavor a delicious meal at a local restaurant offering Sri Lankan or international cuisine with stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

2:30 PM: Visit Hakgala Botanical GardenExplore the Hakgala Botanical Garden, home to a wide variety of exotic plants and flowers. Marvel at its beautifully landscaped lawns and serene ambiance.

3:30 PM: Stroll Through Nuwara Eliya TownTake a brief tour through the town. Visit the famous Post Office, a colonial-era building, or shop for fresh strawberries and local souvenirs

Evening

4:30 PM:Return to KandyBegin your drive back to Kandy, enjoying the sunset views along the way

6:30 PM: Arrival in KandyArrive in Kandy, completing a memorable journey to the hill country’s most charming destination.

Tour Includes

  • Comfortable round-trip transportation
  • Entrance tickets to all attractions English-speaking guide

What to Bring

  • Warm clothing for the cool climate Camera for stunning photos Comfortable shoes for walking
  • Escape to the cool highlands and explore the timeless beauty of Nuwara Eliya, where nature and serenity come together!
