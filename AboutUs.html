About Us

Our Mission

Our mission at Pehesara Lanka Tours is to provide exceptional travel experiences that highlight the best of Sri Lanka. We are dedicated to promoting sustainable tourism, preserving our country's natural and cultural heritage, and supporting local communities. Through personalized service, expert planning, and a deep passion for Sri Lanka, we aim to create lasting memories for every traveler.

Our Vision

To be the foremost tour agency in Sri Lanka, celebrated for delivering authentic, sustainable, and unforgettable travel experiences that deeply connect visitors with the island's rich culture and natural beauty.

Who We Are

We are a passionate team of travel experts, dedicated to curating unforgettable experiences in Sri Lanka, combining local knowledge with a commitment to sustainable tourism.

Why Choose Us

Choose us for our personalized service, expert local knowledge, and dedication to creating unique, authentic, and responsible travel experiences in Sri Lanka.

What We Do

We specialize in creating tailor-made tours that showcase the best of Sri Lanka, from cultural explorations and wildlife encounters to adventure activities and serene beach getaways.
